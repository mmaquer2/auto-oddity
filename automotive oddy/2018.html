2018 Year In Review

2018 was a very memorable year for motorsports, automobiles, and technology in this article we review the most memorable and impactful moments of the past 12 months. Lewis Hamilton won his fifth fomrula one world championship title, Sebastion Ogier made it his sixth WRC title, Scott Dixon adds another championship to his hall of fame resume. Will power finally wins in Indianapolis. And so much more.


Race of the Year: 2018 Azerbaijan Grand Prix

This race had a little bit of everything.

Most interesting new car: The McLaren Speedtail


The mclaren speed tail was announced only a few short weeks ago out of nowhere. This new vehicle from Mclaren automobiles is arguably the true spirtual successor to the the iconic Mclaren F1. It is a center seat driving coupe with a top speed of 250 mph. Filled to the brim with bespoke engineering technology Mclaren is known for. Its Other monster specs state that it will be able to reach 0 to 300 KM/H in 12.8 seconds, also boasting a dry weight of only 1,430 Kilograms. Powered by a 1050 PS hybrid power train. If this is what the future looks like I cant wait.

Best overtake of the year : Daniel Ricciardo Chinese Grand Prix

Ric was a man on a mission during this year's chinese grand prix. He started qualifying in sixth position and moved his way to the front of the field ahead of faster mercedes and ferrari cars in spectacular fashion. He was able to hold off Bottas and Raikkonen for one of the handful of redbull victories this year. Watch this video for a collection of his overtakes that lead him to the win.
